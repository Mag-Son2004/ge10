What?

I attended the Hibalag Christian Night event on August 21 during the Hibalag Festival. The event was held at Ravello Field. Its objective was to bring people together to worship, share the message of God, and celebrate faith through music and fellowship. My role was as an audience member, listening and enjoying the performances. I attended with my friends, while the singers and speakers delivered inspiring messages about God, and the Fellowship Band led the crowd in lively and energetic worship.

So What?

The event deepened my appreciation for the power of music and community in expressing faith. I initially expected a more formal atmosphere, but I was surprised by how lively and engaging it was. The Fellowship Band made a strong impression with their energy and heartfelt songs, creating an uplifting and joyful experience. I plan to apply what I learned by being more active in church and attending similar events in the future. Moving forward, I want to engage more with groups that promote spiritual growth and fellowship. I see the benefits of events like this because they inspire individuals and create a sense of unity. If I could attend this event again, I would participate more actively, perhaps by joining in the singing or discussions.
