What?

On October 30, I attended an event called Pasiklaban Volleyball 9th Game organized by Silliman Senior High School at the Silliman Gymnasium. The game featured senior students competing in their respective teams, Red versus Blue, and was both fun and highly competitive. Me as a spectator, cheering for the Red team alongside other enthusiastic viewers.

So What?

The event was thrilling, especially as the Blue team led in the early rounds. However, the Red team made an incredible comeback in the 3rd and 4th rounds, overtaking the score and eventually winning the game. It was exciting to witness the determination and teamwork of the players. Cheering for the Red team and seeing their victory was a joyful experience that highlighted the spirit of competition and sportsmanship.

Now What?

This experience made me appreciate the effort and energy involved in team sports and encouraged me to support more school events like this. It also inspired me to consider playing or actively participating in similar activities in the future. If I could attend again, I would bring more friends to share the fun and excitement.


Because of this activity, I am more inspired to engage in and support team sports and school activities.
