What?

I attended the Hibalag SILLIMUSIKA 2024 event at Silliman University on August 24 with my friends. The event showcased the best local artists of Dumaguete, celebrating the vibrant music scene in the area. It was an exciting opportunity for me to see my favorite band, Lola Amour, a popular band in the Philippines. The event was lively, with a large crowd enjoying the performances, creating an energetic and unforgettable atmosphere.

So What?

The event left me inspired and energized, especially seeing Lola Amour perform live. I initially expected it to be a fun experience, but the energy of the crowd and the talent of the performers exceeded my expectations. The most surprising part was how engaged everyone was, singing along and truly appreciating the music. I was especially impressed by the performance of Lola Amour, which was energetic and full of passion, making the whole experience even more memorable.

Now What?

This experience reminded me of the power of live music in bringing people together and how events like this can support local artists. I also realized the importance of attending such performances to appreciate the music and support the artists. Moving forward, I plan to attend more local music events and continue supporting bands like Lola Amour. If I could relive the event, I would make sure to get even closer to the stage to enjoy the performances more intimately.


Because of this activity, I am more excited to explore, enjoy more of the music culture and support the local music scene and attend live performances whenever possible.
