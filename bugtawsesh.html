What?

I attended the BUGTAW Session 3, an evening gig held on August 17 at Bugtaw, Dumaguete. The event featured performances from various talented bands, showcasing their skills in playing instruments and singing. As an audience, I was there to enjoy the music and support the local music scene while immersing myself in the lively and creative atmosphere.

So What?

The event inspired me to pursue playing the electric guitar more seriously. Watching the bands perform with such passion and skill made a strong impression on me. I was particularly entertained by how effortlessly they played their instruments and how their voices blended with the music. It was uplifting to see their dedication and how they connected with the audience. The most enjoyable part was feeling the energy of the live performances and the sense of camaraderie among the crowd.

Now What?

This experience motivated me to improve my guitar skills and explore more opportunities to attend live music events. I realized the value of supporting local artists and how their performances can inspire others. Moving forward, I plan to practice more consistently and perhaps even join gigs like BUGTAW in the future. If I could relive the event, I would stay longer and engage with the performers to learn more about their musical journey.


Because of this activity, I am more motivated to develop my guitar talent,to widen my music knowledge and support the local music community.
