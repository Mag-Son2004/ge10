What?

On August 19, I attended the CCS Leaderboards Tournament at Alfredo T. Ang (ATA Hall), organized by the College of Computer Studies. The event featured a Mobile Legends competition, a 5v5 multiplayer MOBA game where teams battled for the winning title. as a spectator, cheering on my friends who were participating, while other teams also showcased their skills and teamwork.

So What?

The tournament was incredibly fun to watch, especially with everyone screaming and cheering for their teams. What stood out to me was the creativity of the team names and how intensely competitive yet entertaining the matches were. I was impressed by how strategically and skillfully the players approached the game, showcasing their talents as gamers. It was exciting to see such energy and passion during the matches.

Now What?

Watching this event gave me a deeper appreciation for eSports and the level of strategy involved in games like Mobile Legends.
