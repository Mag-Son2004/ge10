What?

I attended the Buglasan Festival: Dog Show at Robinsons Mall Dumaguete on October 24 organized by the Provincial Government of Negros Oriental . The event brought together dog lovers who showcased different dog breeds, including country breeds, cross-breeds, and exotic breeds. As I watched and learned, the participants proudly presented their dogs and shared information about their unique traits and care.

So What?

The event amazed me because I was able to see so many different dog breeds in one place. I was surprised to learn about exotic and rare breeds that I had never seen before. The experience expanded my knowledge about dogs and their characteristics, helping me appreciate the diversity and culture within the dog-loving community. The most satisfying part was witnessing how passionate and proud the owners were in showcasing their dogs.

Now What?

This experience gave me a new appreciation for dog culture and the importance of understanding different breeds. Moving forward, I want to learn more about caring for and identifying dog breeds. I also see the value of events like this because they promote responsible pet ownership and connect people with shared interests. If I could attend a similar event again, I would take the opportunity to interact more with the owners and ask questions.


Because of this activity, I am more informed and amazed by the diversity and culture surrounding dogs.
