What?

I attended an event called Book Bingo on August 21 at Alfredo T. Ang Hall (ATA Hall), organized by the SU Library. The session aimed to promote a love for reading, encourage participants to explore diverse literary works, and provide a fun way to test luck and skills. As I participated, enjoying the activities with my friends , It was also really fun . The speakers introduced themselves, shared their ideas about the importance of reading, and organized games to keep the audience engaged.

So What?

The event helped me appreciate how fun and interactive activities like Book Bingo can encourage reading. I expected it to be a simple discussion, but I was surprised by how lively and engaging it was, especially with the games. The speakers made a strong impression with their enthusiasm and efforts to promote reading. The most satisfying part was participating in the game while enjoying a sense of togetherness with my friends and the other attendees. I liked the mix of fun and learning, which made the experience enjoyable and meaningful.

Now What?

This event made me realize the importance of promoting reading in creative ways. I learned that games like Book Bingo can make reading more exciting and accessible for everyone. Moving forward, I plan to explore new books and genres to expand my knowledge. I would also like to attend more activities organized by the library to stay connected and involved. If I could join this event again, I would participate more actively and encourage others to attend as well.


Because of this activity, I am more inspired to read and discover new books while sharing the joy of reading with others.
