What?

On October 12, I attended the Tekken Tournament: Group Stage at Macias Sports Complex, organized by the E-Sport Negrense League. Tekken is a fighting game where players use different characters with unique styles and combos to battle opponents. I was cheering for my friend who participated in the event, while other players showcased their skills in the competition.

So What?

Watching the tournament was incredibly fun and interesting. The players were highly skilled, predicting their opponentsâ€™ moves and exploiting character weaknesses. It was amazing to see such competitive gameplay and the level of mastery involved. The experience also showed me how much understanding, time, and patience it takes to learn the mechanics of Tekken and play effectively.

Now What?

This event gave me a new appreciation for fighting games and the strategy required to excel in them. It inspired me to try playing Tekken and to learn its mechanics in-depth. If I could attend a similar event again, I would take more time to observe how players analyze their opponents and enjoy the competitive atmosphere even more.


Because of this activity, I am more curious about competitive gaming and motivated to explore fighting games like Tekken.
